My Career at The University of Maine

I decided to come to the University of Maine because it fulfilled my wishes to run NCAA Division 1 Track and Field, while also allowing me to pursue my desired major. So far, I am loving it. I am a Multi for the team, which means I do many events. I compete in things like the Hpetathalon and the Decathalon. In addition, my team is full of incredibly talented and smart individuals. I have become close friends with many of them already and I am looking forward to what the future brings. .

Beaton signs NLI to UMO
